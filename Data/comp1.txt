Perf 1 notes on Powers of two Sunday, March 19

We had Dan (Bow and main), Marek (jib), Megan (jib release and spin trim), and Dennis

There were a couple of glitches that were ironed out before racing. The hydrolics on backstay were fixed (tighten set screw on release knob).
The compass display made to display properly. Dan entered marks into GPS.

A nice day... 70 degrees, sunny. Wind was predicted to be NNe to ENE at about 6 kts.
It was actually nirth, perhaps shifting a bit left at 13-17 kts.

Course was Olympi 1 to the north(Triangle windward leeward windward).

prestart, upwind headings were 045 on stbd, 322 on port.

Prestart strgedy was to head right up the beat, with a likely persistant shift to the right. 
Specifically not to engage with Esprit... just sail our own race. 
So, start cleanly and tack pretty quickyly to port.

Esprit had a different plan. Shortly after we tacked to port, they did as well. Se we were able to tune with them a bit. We were often higher or faster...
which was quite wonderful! Unfortunately we weren't able to be able to pull out ehead enough to be able to tack.
So, they rode us out past the starboard tack layline.

Second leg was a tight reach. We decided to not use a chute on the reaches... lack of experience and crew. So, we lost ground to Esprit and Gaucho.

Up the second beat we did ok, gaining perhaps on esprit.

For the run, we did set a chute. All went quite well. We gybed several times without incident. The driver had to be constantly reminded that
he was mot on a J/30 and sailing deep doesn't work well. The leeward rounding took a bit of time, but nothing was damaged.

Final beat was ok. We finished 3rd of 4. It was likely Esprit, Goucho, Powers of two, Arnie?

I was happy with our upwind boat speed. Electronics worked well. Crew all seemed to be concentrating on the race.

Things to work on are downwind driving, downwind mark roundings, leeward takedowns on port tack.
